About Us

This is what we do.

We're food lovers, number crunchers, and meticulous budgeters. We love science and art, and the way they come together when we cook. we love to create, problem solve, and learn new things. We're not a professionals chef or financial expert, but we love learning by doing and sharing our experiences.

The simple act of tracking food costs was transformative. With all the data in front of us, we were able to see where out money was being wasted and where we were getting the most value. we began planning my meals and grocery lists around the budget savers and avoiding the budget sinkers. We started experimenting with new recipes, discovering new foods, and finding new ways to keep our stomachs satisfied while still spending less.
